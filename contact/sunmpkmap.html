Sun Microsystems' Menlo Park Campus
Menlo Park, CA

(650) 786-4889


  • From the South/San Jose take 101 North to the Willow Road exit and go right (East) towards the Dumbarton Bridge until Willow dead-ends into the Sun campus at 84 intersection.
  • From the North/San Francisco take the Marsh Road exit. Go East and make a right when Marsh dead-ends into Bayshore and go towards the Dumbarton Bridge. The Sun campus will be on your left hand side at Willow Road intersection.
